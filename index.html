Blox Fruit Guide

PVE

  • good common fruit
1. smoke smoke fruit

Z : Hits the ground with smoke and launches enemies into the air.

X : Throws smoke thorns to inflict continuous damage.

C : Summons smoke around you, turns opponents with smoke and throws it.

F : Summons smoke wings to fly around, then throws smoke bombs at the ground while flying, dealing damage.



  • good uncommon fruit
2. dark dark fruit

Z : The user fires multiple stones at the location the mouse is pointing at, dealing damage. The damage is pretty strong, but it's very difficult to hit.

X : Summons a black hole at the mouse point, sucks it in, deals damage, and then throws enemies.

C : Opens a medium-sized black hole, dealing continuous damage to anyone who gets sucked into it. Once activated, the user cannot move until the end, so there is a bit of a risk.

V : Creates and throws a large sphere of darkness. If it hits, it sucks people in and finally blows them away.

--------------------------------------------------------------------

It's easy to get so far, and it's a good fruit for PVE, so I recommend it.

--------------------------------------------------------------------

  • rare good fruit


3. light light fruit

Z :Throws a kick of light, dealing wide-area damage.

X : It instantly jumps high and fires light bullets in a fan shape. The longer you press it, the longer it fires.

C : Summons light mirrors in front of you and goes to the designated location and kicks down.

V : Drop light lasers from the sky at a designated location.

F : Turns into light and flies very fast.
